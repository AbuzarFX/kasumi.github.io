Commands of Kasumi
We provide you the best features in Kasumi that a music bot has to offer! Here are the commands of Kasumi.
Play command

Join a voice channel and use &play (song name) to play the song.

Applying Music filters

Play a song and use the command &filter (name of the filter) to apply the filter.

Radio Stations

Play a radio station in the voice channel using the command &radio (1-34)
